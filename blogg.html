My Music taste

Lately, I've been diving deep into my own little world of sound. If you catch me between games or at the piano, you’ll probably hear the melancholic vibes of Radiohead, the eclectic genius of Tyler, the Creator, or the dreamy haze of Beach House floating through the air. Radiohead’s introspective lyrics hit me in the feels every time—Thom Yorke just gets it, you know? Tyler’s wild creativity keeps my mind racing, and Beach House? Those chill, ethereal vibes are the perfect soundtrack for late-night grinds. It’s a mix that keeps me grounded but always ready to pop off when it’s time to play.

Piano skills

When I’m not terrorizing the enemy backline in Overwatch, you can find me at the piano, absolutely shredding. I’ve been playing since I was a kid, and there’s something about the flow of fingers on keys that keeps me coming back. Whether it’s a classical piece, some jazzy improvisation, or just messing around with whatever pops into my head, playing the piano is my way of decompressing. It’s like I’m talking to the keys, and they talk back. I guess you could say music is my second language—one that speaks volumes without a single word.
